About ELYSIUM 2025

ELYSIUM 2025 is an annual symposium that brings together students from colleges across the nation in a healthy and competitive environment. The event is designed to challenge participants, foster innovation, and provide a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and compete with the best minds.

Why Participate?

ELYSIUM 2025 is more than just a competition; it's an experience where technology and fun come together. From mind-bending challenges to interactive activities, we ensure that every participant learns, grows, and enjoys the journey.

What to Expect?

  • Exciting technical and non-technical events
  • Opportunities to showcase your skills
  • Networking with like-minded peers
  • Thrilling competitions with exciting rewards

Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology offers education in undergraduate studies. It has a very able and skilled bunch of professors and researchers. The paramount aim is to offer the highest degree of theoretical and practical knowledge, encoded with a disciplined strength of mind to fully understand the vital sophistication of all technical and software technologies.
